01 · Urea Alternative / Crop Nutrition Support

Uri Fast — Urea

For grains and pulses

An agro-purpose product positioned as an alternative to chemical urea, used for grains and pulses to support overall plant health.

Dose
2 ml in 1 litre of water
Contains
Natmur, Bovista, Nitric Acid, etc.

Key Benefits

  • Suitable for grain and pulse crops
  • Positioned as an alternative to chemical urea
  • Contains essential micronutrients
  • Supports overall plant health
  • Non-chemical and non-toxic as per product label
  • Easy to apply during watering

Application Guidance

After 20–25 days of sowing during first watering · Again after ~40 days during second watering · Third application at any later watering stage.

Only for plants. Not for human or animal consumption.

05 · Pesticide / Pest, Virus & Insect Control

Virosil — Pesticide

For all types of plants and crops

An agro-purpose product formulated for pest, virus and insect-related crop issues. Can be used as preventive support as well as for eradication where pest issues are already present.

Dose
2 ml in 1 litre of water
Contains
Natsulf, Poison Oak, Cuprum Met, etc.

Key Benefits

  • Helps manage pest-related crop problems
  • Suitable for different types of plants and crops
  • Can be used as preventive support
  • Can be used where pest control action is required
  • Non-chemical and non-toxic as per product label
  • Easy to dilute and apply with water

Application Guidance

Direct in soil at seed sowing · 20–25 days after sowing · After ~30 days for pest control · In case of severe pest issues, continuously for 3 days or as required.

Only for plants. Not for human or animal consumption.
