Application Guide

How to use Homeo Agro products.

Easy to use and apply with water — by crop stage and product purpose. Follow recommended dose and timing for best results.

General Dose

A simple, consistent ratio.

For most liquid products, the recommended dose is the same. Mix properly before application and apply as per the crop requirement.

  • Always dilute the product as recommended
  • Use clean water for mixing
  • Apply at the recommended crop stage
  • Follow the instructions on the product label

Crop Stage Timing

The right product, at the right stage.

A general timeline across the crop cycle.

SOWING
Day 0

At seed sowing

Plantowin-D — apply at seed sowing (liquid 2 ml/L or powder mixed with dry clay or sand).
Virosil — may be applied directly in soil.

EARLY
20–25 days

First watering

Uri Fast (grains, pulses) · Plantowin-K (fruits, vegetables, flowers) · Virosil (pest control).

PEST

FINAL

Product-Wise Guide

Detailed usage per product.

01

Virosil — Pesticide

Virosil may be used in the soil at the time of seed sowing. It can also be used 20–25 days after sowing when the plant begins to grow. For pest control, another application may be given after around 30 days. In case of active pest issues, it may be used continuously for 3 days or as required.

02

Uri Fast — Urea Alternative

Uri Fast should be used for grains and pulses. It is normally used after 20–25 days of sowing when the first watering is given. It may be used again after around 40 days during the second watering. A third application can be given at any later watering stage.

03

Plantowin-K — NPK Alternative

Plantowin-K should be used for fruits, vegetables and flowers. It can be applied after 20–25 days of sowing during the first watering and again after around 40 days during the second watering. A third application may be given whenever water is applied.

04

Plantowin-D — DAP Alternative

Plantowin-D should be used at the time of seed sowing. In liquid form, mix 2 ml in 1 litre of water. In powder form, it may be mixed with dry clay or sand before use.

05

Last Tuch — Final Stage Support

Last Tuch should be used at the final plant stage when buds or beans appear. It should be sprayed directly on the plant.

Important Safety

Safe handling matters.

  • Always dilute the product as recommended.
  • Use clean water for mixing.
  • Apply at the recommended crop stage.
  • Do not use for human or animal consumption.
  • Keep away from children.
  • Store in a safe and dry place.
  • Do not mix with unknown chemicals unless advised by an agriculture expert.
  • Follow the instructions mentioned on the product label.

These products are strictly for plant and agricultural use only. They are not medicines for humans or animals and must not be consumed.
