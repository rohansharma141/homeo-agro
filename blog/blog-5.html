Safe Handling of Agro Products — Important Tips for Farmers

Even when a product is non-chemical or non-toxic for plant use, proper handling is important. Good practices protect not only the farmer but also the crop, the soil and the people around the farm.

Read before you mix

Farmers should always read the label before use. The label carries crucial information — dose, timing, crop suitability and safety notes. Products should be diluted correctly, stored safely and kept away from children.

Agro products should not be consumed by humans or animals — regardless of how natural or non-toxic the label describes them.

What "agro use only" means

Homeo Agro products are clearly marked for plant use only. They should be used only for agricultural purposes and according to the recommended dose. This is true even though our products are positioned as non-chemical and non-toxic for plants — the "for plants" qualifier is essential.

Storage basics

Store in a safe and dry place. Avoid direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Keep bottles closed when not in use. Don't transfer the product to unmarked containers — labels matter, especially in shared spaces and households with children.

Mixing rules

Use clean water for mixing. Do not mix with unknown chemicals unless advised by an agriculture expert. If you're not sure whether two products are compatible, ask before mixing.

Safe usage helps protect farmers, crops and the farming environment. Every step counts — from buying the right product to disposing of empty containers responsibly.
