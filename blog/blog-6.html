The Complete Homeo Agro Range

A natural agricultural solution for farmers — an alternative to chemical fertilizers.

An alternative to chemical fertilizers has now arrived with a new solution. This solution has been made possible in the form of homeopathic fertilizer, which works as a natural and organic fertilizer.

In modern agriculture, along with increasing crop production, the protection of soil, plants, the environment, and human health has also become an important subject for everyone. Considering the continuously rising costs, dependence on chemical fertilizers and pesticides, and crop-related problems such as pests, diseases, and nutritional deficiencies, farmers are now looking for alternatives that are affordable, low-cost, capable of supporting higher production, simple to use, helpful for crop growth, and able to make farming safer and more balanced.

Keeping this need in mind, Bala Ji Homeo Agro Solutions, District Hardoi, Uttar Pradesh, has introduced a special range of Homeo Agro products for farmers. These homeopathic fertilizers are prepared only for agricultural and plant use. According to the company, these products can be used to support crop production growth, pest control, virus and insect-related problems, and crop nutrition. The products manufactured by Bala Ji Homeo Agro are non-chemical and non-toxic.

The objective is to provide farmers with products that can serve as an alternative to chemical fertilizers and support different crop needs — from pest control and nutritional support to plant growth and final-stage quality.

A Useful Product Range for the Agriculture Sector

The Homeo Agro range includes several products designed to meet the different needs of farmers. These products can be used at different stages of crop growth — at the time of sowing, 20–25 days after sowing, around 40 days after sowing, during irrigation, and when buds, pods, or fruits start appearing on plants.

According to the company, these products are useful for grains, pulses, fruits, vegetables, flowers, and all types of crops. They can be mixed with water and applied easily in fields, plots, or on plants.

Uri Fast Urea — For Grains and Pulses

Uri Fast Urea has been specially developed for grains and pulses. According to the company, it can be used as an alternative to chemical urea. It contains essential micronutrients, which may support the overall health of plants.

This product can be used approximately 20–25 days after sowing, when the first irrigation is given in the field. After that, it can be used again during the second irrigation at around 40 days. The third application may be given as required during any irrigation. The recommended quantity is 2 ml per 1 litre of water.

Plantowin-K NPK — For Fruits, Vegetables and Flowers

Plantowin-K NPK is especially useful for fruits, vegetables, and flowers. It can be used as an alternative to chemical NPK and contains essential micronutrients. Its purpose is to support the health, growth, and nutrition of all types of plants, fruits, and flowers.

Plantowin-K can be used 20–25 days after sowing during the first irrigation, and again around 40 days later during the second irrigation. The third application may be given as required when water is applied in the field. The normal dosage is 2 ml per 1 litre of water.

Plantowin-D (Liquid) DAP — For All Crops

Plantowin-D (Liquid) DAP has been developed for all types of crops and plants. According to the company, it can be used as an alternative to chemical DAP fertilizer.

This liquid product is used at the time of sowing. The recommended quantity is 2 ml per 1 litre of water, mixed and applied directly in the field.

Plantowin-D (Powder) DAP — For All Crops

Plantowin-D (Powder) DAP is the powder form of the same agro-purpose product, also positioned as an alternative to chemical DAP fertilizer. Suitable for all types of crops and plants.

The powder is used at the time of sowing and can be mixed with dry soil or sand before application in the field.

Virosil Pesticide — For Pest, Virus and Insect-Related Problems

In the Homeo Agro range, Virosil Pesticide has been prepared for all types of pest, virus, and insect-related problems. This product can be used on all types of plants and crops. It can be used for preventive purposes and also as an eradicator where pest-related problems already exist.

Virosil can be applied to the soil at the time of sowing. It can also be used 20–25 days after sowing when the plants start emerging. If the pest problem is severe in the field, it may be used continuously for 3 days or as required. The normal dosage is 2 ml per 1 litre of water.

Last Tuch KP — For the Final Stage of Crops

Last Tuch KP is useful for all crops at the final stage. This product is used when buds or pods start appearing on plants. It may support plant stimulation, fruit development, and better growth.

Last Tuch KP is recommended to be sprayed on plants. Its dosage is also 2 ml per 1 litre of water. This product may be useful for farmers at the final stage of crop development, especially when plants require additional nutrition and growth support.

Easy to Use, Low Dosage and Suitable for Different Crop Stages

One of the main features of Homeo Agro products is that they can be mixed with water and used easily. Most liquid products are recommended at a dosage of 2 ml per 1 litre of water. Farmers can use them according to the condition of their crop and the product instructions.

These products can be used at the following stages:

  • At the time of sowing
  • 20–25 days after sowing
  • Around 40 days after sowing
  • During irrigation
  • When pest problems occur
  • When buds and pods appear
  • At the final growth stage of the crop
